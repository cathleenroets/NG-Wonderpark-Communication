Prayer Requests

At NG Kerk Wonderpark, we are a 7-star congregation committed to worship, healing, and pastoral care. Share your prayer requests with our community, and our dedicated prayer team will lift up your intentions daily in faith and fellowship. Whether seeking comfort or giving thanks, your prayers strengthen our bond as a family in Christ. Need help? Visit our Contact page.

Please keep requests respectful and concise. Private requests are seen only by our prayer team.
